- Added `components/marketplace/DigitalDesignCard.tsx` for reusable design card rendering with image background.
- Added `types/marketplaceDesign.ts` with:

  - Firestore mapping helper
  - defensive field parsing and date/price formatting helpers

4) Placeholder routes maintained for staged rebuild
